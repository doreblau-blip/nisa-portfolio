THE
SYSTEM
IS NOT
BROKEN.

Dore operates as a structural element — a visual system rather than just letterform. Glitches, blocks and repetition become the language of broken continuity. In a world of perfect digital alignment, Dore celebrates the fragment. It is a typeface designed for those who see geometry in noise and balance in the irregular. Every weight tells a story of structural evolution.
