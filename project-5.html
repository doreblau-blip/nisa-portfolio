Saline

2026 branding design

SALINE is a self-initiated branding and visual exploration project developed to push my approach to packaging design, typographic systems, and ai image-making. The project focuses on creating a bold, contemporary identity for a fictional artisanal beer brand made with olives, defined by a dry, salty, and acidic character.
